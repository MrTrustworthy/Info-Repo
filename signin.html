You need to sign in to do that!

In order to submit changes to the Information Repository, you need to sign in. This is just to make sure a real person is doing it, and to help prevent spammy changes. If you don't want to sign in, but still want to say something, please send me an email, and I'll get on to it.

All sign-in is handled by Firebase, not me. I only see what the provider you choose shares by default (usually a name, email address, and picture). I do not see your password, posts, or anything else of a more private nature. Authentication is only used on pages that require it.

As always, the source code for this site is open for anyone to view.
